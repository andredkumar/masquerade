Customize Your Mailchimp Form

Before launching, make sure your Mailchimp form looks good:

    Go back to Audience → Signup forms → Form builder
    Customize:
        ✅ Form title: "Join Masquerade Beta"
        ✅ Button text: "Get Beta Access"
        ✅ Success message: "Thanks! Check your email for next steps."
        ✅ Remove unnecessary fields (keep just Email + First Name)
    Enable double opt-in (recommended for deliverability)
    Set up welcome email (see previous instructions)

What This Looks Like in Practice

Landing Page Flow:

    User scrolls your landing page
    Sees "Join Free Beta" button (linked to your Mailchimp URL)
    Clicks → Opens Mailchimp form in new tab
    Enters email + name → Submits
    Mailchimp sends automated welcome email
    User gets started

Downsides of this approach:
